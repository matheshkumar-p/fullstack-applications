Our History

Banana Leaf Restaurant takes pride to introduce itself as one of the leading Authentic High Class A/C Restaurant in Boston, USA. Banana Leaf Restaurant was established during late 1990’s and on existence for several years. We have two branches in Boston and extend our service in San Diego & Hawai.

We have attracted thousands of customers by providing them healthy and tasty foods. We have more than 300 employees ready to serve your needs at the most. We have talented chefs to provide healthy foods which you have ever tasted in and around the city. We have specialized in Millet Foods. We ensure our customers are our strength and make them feel at home when they are with us. An outlet to be great with friends or all by yourself. You can eat in, take it to go, or have it catered right to you. The feeling is friendly and the food is fabulous. Whenever you need it, wherever you need it, it is your own unique world to eat out.

We offer Party Hall for free on your special occasions such as Birthday parties, Reception, family - friend’s get-together, Official-Business meeting, etc… Our Party Hall is spacious to more than 300 accommodate with fulfilling all your needs. We do offer food packages on your occasions to make it more special. We have packages for Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner on affordable prices. We ensure our food satisfy not only your budget also your taste buds.
